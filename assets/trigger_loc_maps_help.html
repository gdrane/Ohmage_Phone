You can add one or more locations to this place in three ways:
  • Press and hold any location on the map.
  • Use the Search option from the menu to look up a street address (Note: You can search only using street addresses and not using business names).
  • Use the My location option from the menu to find your current location which can be added by tapping on the blue dot.
While defining locations around buildings, use the following tips for for best results:
  • Place the marker at the center of the building instead of placing it on the street. (Note: The search option mostly shows the points on the street instead of the center of the building).
  • Click and drag the red circle around the flag to make it large enough to cover the entire building.
  • You can also place multiple flags at various corners of the building to cover all entry points to the building.
